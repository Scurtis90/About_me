About Me

Born and raised in Utah. I spent my younger years playing lots of sports with some video games sprinkled in. In high school I was on the basketball team and played trumpet in the band. I eventually served an LDS church mission in Riverside, California. Afterwards, I got started in the electrical trade and met my future wife.


My life is currently defined by my 7 year marriage. We do just about everything together. From movie critiquing to hiking to fantasy football, my free time is spent almost entirely with my wife. We have visited every national park in Utah numerous times and are trying to make time for all the state parks as well. We are both diehard Minnesota Vikings fans, so we have made a couple trips over there too.
